    What Is A Smoothie?

    Smoothies are beverages made by blending ingredients together. Smoothies normally have a liquid base, such as fruit juice, milk, yogurt and even ice. Other ingredients include food such as real fruit and or vegetables. Nutritional supplements can be added as well. Smoothies can very nutritional and healthy. Including healthy juices and vegtables is a great way to include important foods into your overall health.
