    Ingredients

    The type of ingredients that you might consider to make your smoothies is totally up to you since smoothies can be of many flavors. The type of ingredients one might want to start out using when making a smoothie that is affordable and healthy is the strawberry and banana smoothie. The strawberry and banana smoothie consist of blending frozen strawberries with a fresh banana with a little bit of a liquid of your choice such as milk or fruit juice. Frozen berries are much cheaper than fresh berries and still contain the same vitamins and minerals. The carrot and apple smoothie is also a great choice when it comes to a healthy and affordable smoothie. It is one of the healthiest and affordable smoothies you can make. Just a simple blend of some carrots and apples with water or a little bit of jiuce will do the trick to this amazingly tasty and nutricious smoothie. Another healthy and affordable smoothie is the banana breakfast smoothie. This smoothie is pretty simple to make. Simply just blend a single banana, half cup of oats and some liquids such as water, juice, milk, iced coffee or any other liquid of your choice. Oats are afforable and a great option when it comes to your health. Smoothies can be made of anything you would like.
